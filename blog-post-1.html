Why “submit a ticket” beats “book a consult”

The fastest path is triage-first, not calendar-first.

Security delays usually come from figuring out who to call and what to say. A ticket captures the facts fast: urgency, scope, impacted systems, and timelines.

That means you get routed to the right expert immediately—without scheduling delays and long discovery calls.

Ready? Submit a cyber ticket.
