Why law firms, medical practices, and SMBs can no longer afford to treat cybersecurity as an afterthought

Small businesses are increasingly becoming primary targets for ransomware, phishing, business email compromise, credential theft, compliance failures, and operational disruption. Law firms protect sensitive client records. Medical organizations manage regulated patient information. Growing businesses depend heavily on cloud platforms, email systems, backups, and digital operations. Attackers understand this. Here’s why cybersecurity has become a business requirement — not just an IT problem — for legal, healthcare, and small-to-medium businesses.
